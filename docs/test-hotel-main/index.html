Hotel Details

The Soneva Jani Maldives

Price Per Night : $ 9000

"Why is the Soneva Jani one , of the best hotels in Maldives!"

At the heart of a 5.6-kilometre lagoon, Soneva Jani is one of the world’s most exclusive and lowest-density resorts, Located on the island of Medhufaru in the Maldives’ Noonu Atoll, the resort has eight island and 51 over-water villas, split between the North Jetty and South Jetty each.offering inimitable luxury, signature Barefoot Guardian service and uninterrupted ocean viewsx, Discover transformative wellness experiences at Soneva Soul, 13 fine dining destinations and all-inclusive luxury with Soneva Unlimited, alongside aquatic fun and movies under the start.Celebrate the festive season in style thanks to the Soneva Stars calendar featuring exclusive events with Mikaël Silvestre, Timothy Bilton, Paul and many more.
