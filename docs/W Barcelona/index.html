Hotel Details

W Barcelona, Spain

Why is W Barcelona one of the best hotels in Spain?

Situated on the beachfront along the famous Barceloneta boardwalk, W Barcelona is an architectural marvel, combining contemporary luxury with iconic design. Designed by world-renowned architect Ricardo Bofill, this vibrant destination offers panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and the city skyline. Featuring 473 stylish rooms and suites, including 67 luxurious suites and the Extreme WOW Suite, W Barcelona sets the stage for a glamorous escape. Guests can indulge in world-class dining at FIRE, SALT, and ECLIPSE, or rejuvenate at the serene W Spa. With a rooftop pool, direct beach access, and vibrant nightlife, W Barcelona transforms every moment into an unforgettable experience. Celebrate the festive season with exclusive events, live DJ performances, and a chic atmosphere that captures the essence of Barcelona's cosmopolitan energy.
