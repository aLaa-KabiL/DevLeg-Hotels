Katikies, Greece

Why is Katikies one of the best hotels in Santorini ?

Perched atop the cliffs of Oia, Katikies is one of the most iconic and luxurious resorts in Santorini. Offering breathtaking views of the Caldera and the Aegean Sea, the hotel is renowned for its whitewashed architecture, elegant ambiance, and impeccable service. With its infinity pools, fine dining experiences, and exclusive wellness offerings, Katikies delivers an unforgettable blend of sophistication and relaxation. Guests can enjoy romantic sunsets, serene accommodations, and a unique sense of privacy in one of the most picturesque locations in the world.

Room 1 : Suites

Stylishly designed Superior Suite with a King Bed, featuring a private balcony and plunge pool, perfect for soaking in the iconic views of the Caldera.

Room 2 : Pool view

Luxurious comfort with stunning views of the infinity pools and the sea. This spacious suite offers a King Bed, 50" HDTV, and refined Cycladic decor.

Room 3 : Ocean view

Wake up to sweeping views of the Caldera in our Junior Suite with 1 King Bed. Includes a private sitting area, perfect for a serene and romantic escape.

Room 4 : Family rooms

Perfect for families, our Superior King Room combines contemporary elegance with traditional Santorini charm, offering serene courtyard views and 1 King-sized bed.

Room 5 : Honeymoon rooms

Designed for romance, our Honeymoon Suites feature a private terrace with a jacuzzi and a Queen-sized bed, offering an intimate retreat with unforgettable sunset views..

Room 6 : Non-smoking rooms

Ideal for couples or friends, with 2 Double Beds. Enjoy luxurious interiors and private terraces, ensuring comfort and style for up to four guests.
