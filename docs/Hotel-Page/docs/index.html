Hotel Details

Exciting and memorable tours!

Katikies, Greece

"Discover the magic of Greece: an unforgettable adventure between islands,
beaches and ancient history!"

Prepare for a 6 days tour of Greece, where you will immerse yourself in its ancient history, beauty and decision-making.Your adventure begins inland, the city of the gods, where you will walk through the corridors of the Acropolis and explore its ancient neighborhoods that are full of love. From there, Then head to the island of Naxos, to discover its beauty and quiet mountainous terrain, and visit its ancient temples that tell the stories of the civilization of its people. After that, you will move to the jewel of the Aegean Sea, Santorini, where captivating sunsets await you, white houses perched on the slopes of the Caldera.

Day 1: Arrival in Athens

-Arrive at Athens International Airport and check in to your hotel.
-Spend the evening exploring the Plaka neighborhood with its charming streets, shops, and traditional Greek tavernas.

Day 2: Athens Sightseeing

-Start your day with a visit to the Acropolis to see the Parthenon and other ancient ruins. Follow up with a visit to the Acropolis Museum.
-Explore the Ancient Agora and then head to Monastiraki Square for some shopping and dining.

Day 3: Travel to Paros

-Take a morning ferry from Athens (Piraeus) to Paros.
-Arrive in Parikia, the capital of Paros, and explore the town. Visit the Panagia Ekatontapiliani Church and the Archaeological Museum.

Day 4: Paros Beaches and Villages

-Spend the morning at Golden Beach, a famous spot for windsurfing. Visit the fishing village of Piso Livadi for lunch.

Day 5: Travel to Naxos

-Take a short ferry ride from Paros to Naxos.
-Check in to your hotel and explore Naxos Town. Visit the Portara (Temple of Apollo) and the Venetian Castle.

Day 6: Naxos Island Tour

-Rent a car or join a tour to visit the mountain villages of Halki, Filoti, and Apeiranthos. Each village has its own unique charm and history.
-Visit the Temple of Demeter, an ancient sanctuary located in the heart of Naxos.
